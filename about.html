Our Story

Our founder, John TipToe started out as a cobbler and shoeshine on the streets of Sydney. He worked his way up and eventually started his own shoe stall which would eventually become TipToe Soles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide you with the best quality shoes at the best possible prices for years to come, while maintaining a sustainable and zero-carbon footprint so that the Earth remains our home.

Our Future

The future is untold, but we have big plans for the expansion of our physical stores and some additional big news coming very soon. Watch this space for free gifts and other promotions for our loyal supporters and customers!
